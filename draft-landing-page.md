## Problem

Most trading automation jumps from signal to capital too quickly. Backtests can look convincing, bots can overfit, and users often do not know why a trade was skipped or whether a strategy has enough evidence.

## Solution

AegisTrade creates an evidence layer between signal and execution. It observes candidates, records blockers, reviews future outcomes, and separates good rejects from missed opportunities.

## How It Works

Signal -> Shadow Evidence -> Outcome Review -> Readiness Gate -> Controlled Execution.

## Evidence-First Process

- Capture signal candidates.
- Track public-shadow outcomes.
- Review maturity by horizon.
- Measure public API health.
- Explain no-trade reasons.
- Advance only through explicit gates.

## Who It Is For

- Serious retail and semi-pro traders.
- Trading communities that value transparency.
- Strategic partners interested in trading infrastructure.
- Future professional users after stronger evidence.

## Who It Is Not For

- Anyone seeking guaranteed returns.
- Anyone wanting passive income claims.
- Users expecting live account onboarding today.
- Operators who want to skip safety gates.

## Safety-Gated Roadmap

1. Public-shadow evidence.
2. Evidence review.
3. Dashboard/evidence certificate.
4. Controlled readiness decision.
5. Later private beta or professional packaging only after proof.
